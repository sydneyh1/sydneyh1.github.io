Who Will You Meet?

Any fairytale that you could imagine live in Storybrooke but listed below are just a few of the highlights...

Snow White and Prince Charming

Snow White and her husband live with their daughter, Emma, and grandson Henry in the heart of town. If something important is happening in STorybrooke (which is always the case) you're sure to see all for of them there.

Rumplestiltskin and Belle

Rumplestiltskin, notorious for his deals and wealh, lives with his girlfriend, Belle above his/Mr. Gold's shop. Be aware of this trickster and the contracts he may get you to sign, but, if Belle is by his side, he should remain fairly calm and civilized.

The Evil Queen

The Queen is the self-proclaimed mayor of Storybrooke and, as such, she lives in the mayor's mansion. You can't miss it or the apple trees that she has growing out front...
