What should you expect to see at Storybrooke?

insert interview powerpoint

The Toll Bridge

Storybrooke's Toll Bridge marks the location of the magical "troll bridge" where Snow White and Prince James came together to fight the trolls. It was also where David Nolan ("Prince James" today) awoke from his coma. Anything important that happens in Storybrooke more than likely happens at the Toll Bridge, so don't miss out!

Clock Tower

For 28 years, time stood still at 8:15 but when Emma Swan came to town, time resumed and the clock began to move again. Also the location of Rumpelstiltskin's hiding place for his most valued possession. Think you can find it? Visit the clock tower to find out!

Mr. Gold's Pawn Shop

Whether you're after a souvenir for your trip or proof that everything and everyone in Storybrooke is deeply connected, Mr. Gold's Pawn Shop will not dissapoint.
