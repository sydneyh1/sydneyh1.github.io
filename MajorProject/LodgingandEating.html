Granny's Bed and Breakfast

Granny's Bed and Breakfast is a family owned and operated business. Located on main street and within walking distance to the famous clock tower, this cozy little cottage-style inn has accomodations fit for young and old. There are also specialty rooms, such as the room where Emma Swan stayed when she came to town, or the room where Pinocio did his work on Henry's book. Granny and her granddaughter Ruby single-handedly dominate the hotel industry in Storybrooke so if you're looking for a place to stay, Granny's your gal!

Amenities include:

  • Linens, sheets, blankets, towels
  • Heating and Air Conditioning
  • Washer and Dryer
  • High-speed wifi
  • Complimentary breakfast at Granny's Diner!

    Granny's Diner

    It just so happens that Granny and Ruby also control the food and beverage industry in town. Their diner is in the same building as their lodging accomodations and almost all locals are regulars. A lunch at Granny's is a sure way to meet some of your favorite fairytales. Maybe even get the chance to talk over a mug of Granny's famous hot chocolate.
